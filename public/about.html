Requirements for being a partner

  • Implementation of 2 happy customers who uses the type of solution, RPA, Robots or something else, you want to sell under the partnership.
  • A Partner pays a yearly fee for being able to promote as a partner with our trademark.

What does a partner get

  • A partner gets an official OpenIAP partner badge
  • Access to marketing collateral
  • Listed as a partner on our website
  • Lead qualification and matching
  • Sales support
  • Training
